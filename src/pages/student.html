Frequently asked questions

How can I participate in the event?

To join, you need a verified school email that hasn’t been used in past events, and your account must not have participated before. If you previously participated and canceled your lifelong discount, you can’t register again. However, if you haven’t received the $99 lifetime discount, you can register as long as your current plan isn’t at the $99 rate. Most ".edu" emails are verified automatically, but if your school isn’t verified, you can submit a ticket with your school email and website, we will verify the school promptly.

How can I achieve the goal and subscribe for $99?

If 50 or more people from your school sign up, you can purchase the subscription for $99. If your school doesn’t reach 50 sign-ups by the deadline, you’ll get a full refund. The sooner your school reaches 50 sign-ups, the sooner your premium subscription will activate. After reaching 50 participants, others from your school can still get the $99 price until the event ends.

What happens if I currently have an active subscription?

If you have an active premium subscription, registering for the student discount will extend your subscription by an additional year for $99. The student discount offers the same benefits as the regular premium subscription, with a lower price.

How can I win the prizes for the referral event?

You can win prizes by referring friends to join LeetCode, even if you don’t join the back-to-school event. Everyone gets an exclusive referral link, and rewards are based on the number of successful sign-ups through your link. Check out this post for the rewards and rules.
